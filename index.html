Welcome to the 12th Annual IEEE SL SYW Congress

Join us on November 3rd, 4th, and 5th for the most anticipated event of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section. This year's theme, 'Dreams Conquer Boundaries,' encapsulates our commitment to showcasing diverse opportunities, fostering innovation, and addressing global challenges. Explore four dynamic phases - 'Dream Big, Embrace Tech,' 'Diverse Voices, Collective Triumph,' 'Claim Your Boundless Future,' and 'New Challenge? Climate Change?' - as we empower you to dream big, embrace technology, and conquer boundaries.

Where dreams take center stage

Explore our remarkable event venue, the canvas for groundbreaking ideas and boundless ambitions.

Nestled in a charming fishing village along Sri Lanka's northeastern coast, the Carolina Beach Hotel, Chilaw will host the IEEE SL SYW Congress 2023. The hotel offers cosy accommodations, mouthwatering food and a stunning beachfront, promising an unparalleled Congress experience. This picturesque location provides the ideal landscape for collaboration, networking, and personal growth, and the sun, sand, and sea is bound to inspire your journey towards realising your dreams and conquering boundaries.
