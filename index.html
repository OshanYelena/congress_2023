Welcome to the 12th Annual IEEE SL SYW Congress

Join us on November 3rd, 4th, and 5th for the most anticipated event of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section. This year's theme, 'Dreams Conquer Boundaries,' encapsulates our commitment to showcasing diverse opportunities, fostering innovation, and addressing global challenges. Explore four dynamic phases - 'Dream Big, Embrace Tech,' 'Diverse Voices, Collective Triumph,' 'Claim Your Boundless Future,' and 'New Challenge? Climate Change?' - as we empower you to dream big, embrace technology, and conquer boundaries.
