About Us

Welcome to the IEEE SL SYW Congress 2023, the 12th instalment of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section’s flagship event! Taking place on the 3rd, 4th and 5th November, this year's theme is 'Dreams Conquer Boundaries.' Join us for a transformative experience where we celebrate diversity, inspire innovation, and address global challenges.

About The Event

The IEEE SL SYW Congress is the pinnacle of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section, uniting minds from 21 universities. With a 12-year history, it is a catalyst for innovation and a spark for change. Themed 'Dreams Conquer Boundaries,' the IEEE SL SYW Congress 2023 is a three-day journey exploring the phases 'Dream Big, Embrace Tech,' 'Diverse Voices, Collective Triumph,' 'Claim Your Boundless Future,' and 'New Challenge? Climate Change?'. Join us for invaluable networking opportunities, enlightening discussions, and knowledge sharing. This is where dreams flourish, tech thrives, and boundaries yield.

IEEE SL SYW Congress Highlights

Throughout its history, the IEEE SL SYW Congress has been a pioneering platform, uniting professionals and enthusiasts alike, providing inspiration and opportunities to young undergraduates in engineering. Despite challenges, it has thrived, even in innovative virtual formats, ensuring accessibility for all. Each edition focuses on central themes, fostering discussions on disruptive innovation and sustainable tech solutions, all while championing diversity, inclusion, and the limitless potential of young minds in shaping a brighter technological future.

Dream big, embrace tech, and conquer boundaries with us at the IEEE SL SYW Congress 2023. Join our diverse community of innovators from the 3rd to 5th November.
